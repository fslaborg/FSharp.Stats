EVD m

Full Usage: EVD m

Parameters:
    m : 'a

Returns: 'b

Compoutes for an N-by-N real nonsymmetric matrix A, the eigenvalue decomposition eigenvalues and right eigenvectors. The right eigenvector v(j) of A satisfies A * v(j) = lambda(j) * v(j) where lambda(j) is its eigenvalue. The computed eigenvectors are normalized to have Euclidean norm equal to 1 and largest component real. Uses the LAPACK subroutine dgeev with arguments JOBVR = 'V' and JOBVL = 'N' Returns the real (first array) and imaginary (second array) parts of the eigenvalues and a matrix containing the corresponding eigenvectors

m : 'a
Returns: 'b
