center m

Full Usage: center m

Parameters:
    m : matrix

Returns: matrix

Normalizes each feature by substracting the corresponing mean followed by a division by its standard deviation. The centered features of the matrix are centered around 0 and possess a standard deviation of 1. Expects a data matrix with rows representing observations and columns representing features.

m : matrix
Returns: matrix
